The Properties of Bitcoin

To understand why Bitcoin is revolutionary, we must first look at its properties. Unlike government-issued money, Bitcoin is the first digital, global, decentralized money in human history.

1. Absolute Scarcity

There will only ever be 21 million Bitcoin. No king, president, or central bank can print more. This makes it "hard money"â€”wealth that cannot be diluted by inflation.

2. Decentralization

Bitcoin has no "headquarters." It is run by thousands of independent computers (nodes) across the globe. No single person can shut it down or change the rules.

3. Censorship Resistance

Because there is no central authority, nobody can stop you from sending Bitcoin to someone else. It is "permissionless" money.

"Bitcoin is the first system where we have a known, fixed supply that is verified by everyone and controlled by no one."

4. Portability & Divisibility

You can carry $1 billion in your pocket on a thumb drive. One Bitcoin can be divided into 100,000,000 smaller units called Satoshi (or "Sats").
