Lightning Network

The Airport Analogy

Bitcoin is like the big runways and cargo planes. It is secure, but too slow and expensive for small things like a cup of coffee.

The Courier Service

Lightning is like the airport courier. Instead of a cargo jet, you set up a "terminal" (a channel) between people. You can send thousands of small packages back and forth instantly for almost nothing.

You only use the blockchain twice: to open the terminal and to close it when you settle the final bill.

Basic Concepts

The Bar Tab Concept

When you go to a bar, you don't pay for every beer individually. That would be slow and clutter your bank statement.

Opening the Tab

Opening a bar tab is like an on-chain Bitcoin transaction. You lock your card at the desk.

Technical Fundamentals

Byzantine Generals Problem

How do distant armies agree on a plan if they don't trust each other and some might be traitors? This is the core problem Bitcoin solves.

The Consensus Solution

Satoshi used "Proof of Work." To send a valid message, a general must solve a massive puzzle that costs real energy. This makes lying too expensive and proves to everyone else that the message is real.
