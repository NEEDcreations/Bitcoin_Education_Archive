2. How We Use Your Data

  • Display your rank and username on the leaderboard
  • Track your progress, points, streaks, badges, and ticket earnings
  • Process referrals and verify referral qualifications (2,100+ points)
  • Determine giveaway eligibility based on Orange Ticket count
  • Send sign-in magic links to verify your email
  • Send optional push notifications (only if you opt in)
  • Power Nacho's personalized greetings (using your username only)
  • Sync your favorites, read progress, and badges across devices
  • Deliver direct messages between users
  • Display online/away/offline status on profiles and leaderboard
  • Display your public profile (username, level, badges, points) to other users
  • Process and investigate user reports for abuse, scams, or harassment
  • Enforce block lists to prevent unwanted communication
  • Power the PlebTalk community forum (posts, replies, upvotes)
  • Power the LightningMart marketplace (listings, buyer-seller communication)

3. User Profiles

When you create an account, a public profile is visible to other signed-in users. Your profile displays:

  • Username, level, and points
  • Badge count and channel progress
  • Orange Ticket count and streak
  • Online/away/offline status
  • Forum post count and join date

Your email, sign-in provider, and blocked list are never visible to other users.

4. Data Storage

Server-side data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore), hosted in the United States. Firebase is operated by Google and subject to Google's enterprise security practices and certifications.

Client-side data is stored in your browser's localStorage and in the PWA's service worker cache. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted to us.

Direct messages and forum posts are stored in Firebase Firestore. Messages are not end-to-end encrypted. We may access message content when investigating abuse reports.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or monetize your data with anyone. Data visible to other users includes:

  • Your username, points, level, badges, and online status on the public leaderboard and user profiles
  • Your posts and replies in the PlebTalk forum
  • Your marketplace listings and seller name on LightningMart
  • Your direct messages are visible only to you and the recipient

7. Cookies & Local Storage

We do not use tracking cookies. Firebase Authentication may set necessary session cookies for sign-in functionality. Your browser's localStorage is used extensively to store preferences, Nacho interaction data, block lists, and cached progress. The PWA service worker caches site assets for offline access.

8. Nacho (AI Mascot) Privacy

  • Questions you ask Nacho are first processed in your browser using keyword matching
  • If no local answer is found, questions may be sent to our Cloudflare Worker proxy for AI-assisted answers — only the question text is sent, with no user identity attached
  • Nacho Mode chat history is stored only in your browser's localStorage
  • Question history (last 100 questions) is stored locally for analytics and improvement
  • Nacho uses your username to personalize greetings — no other personal data is accessed
  • Nacho's friendship levels, trivia scores, and closet items are all stored locally
  • Live data (price, fees) is fetched from the public mempool.space API — no user data is sent in these requests

9. Messaging & Communication Safety

  • Direct messages are scanned client-side for known scam patterns and suspicious links
  • Flagged messages display a warning to the recipient — we do not prevent delivery of flagged messages
  • You can block any user to prevent them from messaging you
  • You can report users for spam, scams, harassment, inappropriate content, or impersonation
  • Reports are stored in Firebase and reviewed by site administrators
  • Blocked user lists are synced to Firebase for cross-device consistency
  • Messages are sanitized (HTML stripped) before storage and display

10. Your Rights

You can:

  • Export your data — use the "Export My Data" button in Settings → Data
  • Delete your account — use the "Delete My Account" button in Settings → Data (permanently removes all server-side data including messages, posts, and listings)
  • Clear local data — clear your browser's localStorage/cache to remove all client-side data
  • Change your username — in Settings → Account
  • Block users — from any user profile or DM window
  • Report users — from any user profile, DM window, or marketplace listing
  • Sign out — at any time from Settings → Account
  • Disable Nacho — in Settings → Prefs (stops all Nacho interactions and data collection)
  • Disable sounds — in Settings → Prefs
  • Disable push notifications — in Settings → Prefs or your browser settings

When you delete your account, all your data is permanently removed from our Firebase database, including messages, posts, listings, and reports. Local browser data must be cleared separately.

11. Children

The Site is educational and suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The direct messaging feature is available only to registered users. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided personal information or been contacted inappropriately, please contact us immediately and we will take action.

12. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Firebase security rules restricting data access
  • OAuth 2.0 for third-party authentication
  • Email verification required for email-based sign-ins
  • Rate limiting on sign-in attempts and message sending (30 messages/hour)
  • Profanity and scam content filtering on user inputs
  • HTML sanitization on all user-generated content
  • Client-side scam and phishing link detection in messages
  • User blocking and reporting system

13. Data Retention

Account data is retained as long as your account exists. Direct messages are retained as long as both participants' accounts exist. Forum posts and marketplace listings are retained indefinitely unless deleted by the author or removed by administrators. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently deleted from Firebase. Anonymous visit data (visitor ID and count) may be retained for aggregate statistics but contains no personally identifiable information.
