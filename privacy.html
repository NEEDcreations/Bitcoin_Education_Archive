Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

Our commitment: We do not sell, share, or monetize your data. Ever.

We collect the bare minimum needed to power your experience. No tracking, no ads, no third-party analytics.

1. What We Collect

When you create an account, we store:

  • Username — chosen by you, displayed on the leaderboard
  • Email — only if you provide one or sign in via a provider (Google, Twitter, etc.)
  • Points & progress — channels visited, quest scores, streak data
  • Orange Tickets — ticket count and referral information
  • Preferences — theme, language, notification settings

We do not collect your real name, location, IP address, browsing history outside the Site, or any financial information.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Display your rank and username on the leaderboard
  • Track your progress, streaks, and ticket earnings
  • Process referrals and giveaway eligibility
  • Send sign-in magic links (if you use email sign-in)
  • Send optional notifications (only if you opt in)

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore), hosted in the United States. Firebase is operated by Google and subject to Google's security practices. Some preferences are stored locally in your browser (localStorage).

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication — for account sign-in (Google, Twitter/X, GitHub, Facebook, email)
  • Firebase Firestore — for data storage
  • GitHub Pages — for website hosting

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, ad networks, or any tracking/analytics services.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with anyone. Period. Your data is only used to power features on this Site.

6. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. Firebase Authentication may set necessary session cookies for sign-in functionality. Your browser's localStorage is used to store preferences (theme, favorites, visited channels).

7. Your Rights

You can:

  • Export your data — use the "Export My Data" button in Settings → Data
  • Delete your account — use the "Delete My Account" button in Settings → Data
  • Change your username — in Settings → Account
  • Sign out — at any time from Settings → Account

When you delete your account, all your data is permanently removed from our database.

8. Children

The Site is educational and suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

10. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email needcreations@gmail.com.
