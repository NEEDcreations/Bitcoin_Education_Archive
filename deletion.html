How to Delete Your Data

Option 1: Delete from the app

  1. Open bitcoineducation.quest
  2. Sign in to your account
  3. Go to Settings (‚öôÔ∏è in the bottom bar)
  4. Scroll to the bottom and click "Delete Account"
  5. Type DELETE to confirm

This permanently deletes your account and all associated data including your profile, points, badges, tickets, referrals, and forum posts.

Option 2: Email us

Send an email to support@603btc.com with the subject "Delete My Data" and we will delete all data associated with your account within 30 days.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your user profile and username
  • Points, rank, and streak data
  • Orange Tickets and referral history
  • Badges and quest progress
  • Forum posts
  • Push notification tokens
  • Any data linked to your Facebook, Google, Twitter, or GitHub login

What We Don't Store

We do not store your Facebook password, access tokens, or private social media data. We only receive your name/email for authentication purposes.
