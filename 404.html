Lightning Network

The Airport Analogy

Bitcoin is like the big runways and cargo planes. It is secure, but too slow and expensive for small things like a cup of coffee.

The Courier Service

Lightning is like the airport courier. Instead of a cargo jet, you set up a "terminal" (a channel) between people. You can send thousands of small packages back and forth instantly for almost nothing.

You only use the blockchain twice: to open the terminal and to close it when you settle the final bill.

Basic Concepts

The Bar Tab Concept

When you go to a bar, you don't pay for every beer individually. That would be slow and clutter your bank statement.

Opening the Tab

Opening a bar tab is like an on-chain Bitcoin transaction. You lock your card at the desk.

Technical Fundamentals

Byzantine Generals Problem

How do distant armies agree on a plan if they don't trust each other and some might be traitors? This is the core problem Bitcoin solves.

The Consensus Solution

Satoshi used "Proof of Work." To send a valid message, a general must solve a massive puzzle that costs real energy. This makes lying too expensive and proves to everyone else that the message is real.

Node Operations

6 Reasons to Run a Node

Don't trust, verify. Running your own node is the only way to truly use Bitcoin without permission.

  • Privacy: Nobody knows which addresses belong to you.
  • Sovereignty: You enforce the rules of the network yourself.
  • Security: Verify that the coins you received are actual Bitcoin.
  • Network Strength: You help propagate valid blocks to others.
  • Development: Essential for building apps on top of Bitcoin.
  • Lightning: Required to route payments and earn routing fees.
Lightning Network

Lightning Channel Close States

How does a channel end? There are three main ways:

  • Collaborative Close: Both parties agree to close. Fastest and cheapest (lowest fees).
  • Force Close (Unilateral): One party closes without the other. Funds are locked for a "timelock" period for security.
  • Penalty Close (Breach): If one party tries to cheat with an old balance, the other party can take 100% of the funds in the channel.
Technical Fundamentals

Replace-By-Fee (RBF)

Stuck transaction? RBF allows you to "bump" the fee of a pending transaction to get it confirmed faster.

How it works

You broadcast a new version of the same transaction but with a higher fee. Miners will see the higher profit and prioritize the new version, effectively cancelling the old stuck one.
